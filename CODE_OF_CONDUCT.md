# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**, as its code
of conduct. The full text is available at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.

A short summary, in our own words:

- Be welcoming and patient with newcomers.
- Use respectful, inclusive language.
- Disagree on ideas, not on people.
- Assume good faith; ask before accusing.
- No harassment, no discrimination, no personal attacks.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code, please
contact the maintainer privately at **hacharya@u.rochester.edu**, or file
a confidential report through GitHub's "Report content" feature on the
relevant comment, issue, or pull request.

All reports will be handled discreetly. We will not retaliate against
people who report in good faith.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may warn, edit, or remove comments, commits, and
contributions that violate this code; in serious or repeated cases, they
may temporarily or permanently ban a contributor from the project.
Enforcement decisions follow the
[Community Impact Guidelines](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/#enforcement-guidelines)
in the Contributor Covenant.
